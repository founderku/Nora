What is Sheet Metal?

Sheet metal is flat-rolled steel supplied in large sheets or coils, available in a range of thicknesses and finishes.

Typical Applications

Sheet metal is a base material for laser cutting, plasma cutting, and bending work across construction, automotive, and general fabrication.
